About Us

Where Beauty Meets Serenity

Maya Beauty Spa & Fitness is a luxury ladies-only beauty salon located in the heart of Doha, Qatar. We offer all services of a wellness day spa to ensure you leave fully relaxed and pampered.

Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to providing personalized beauty and wellness treatments using the finest products and techniques from around the world. From traditional Moroccan baths to cutting-edge Korean head spas, we bring the best of global beauty to Doha.
